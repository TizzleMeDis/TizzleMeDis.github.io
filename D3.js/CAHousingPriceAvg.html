Housing price bar chart

This bar chart covers California housing prices that span over the last 20 years. It shows the top 12 cities that are priced the highest. You can scroll to see the prices fluxuate overtime.

You can scroll to change the yearly average prices here. Or just watch the animation of the prices.


The data is from a csv file that shows the performace of the market in houses and other data that are incentives to purchase these homes.

Credit: Average Price of Houses Sold in California Throughout the Years by kennynguyen
